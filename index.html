Divine Diamonds, a family-owned jewelry store, had thousands of pieces and no way for customers to browse them online. Every product had to be manually entered. Photos, descriptions, categories, pricing. The owner was spending close to 5 minutes per item, and there was no website at all.

What I Built

Anyone on the team takes a photo from their phone. The product details, category, and description are filled in automatically. They just confirm and it's listed.
